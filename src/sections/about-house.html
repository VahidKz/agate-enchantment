The House of Agate

Our work begins before the first cut.

We are interested in the moment before stone becomes standardized, when it is still read for raw character, latent beauty, and future architectural potential.

Whether presented as a monumental block, an expressive slab, or a carefully sourced selection for a private project, the material is approached as cultural as much as physical.

We do not simply present stone.
We frame the eye through which it is chosen.
