What You Can Contact Us For

Not every inquiry begins with a finished specification.

Some conversations begin with a material. Others begin with a feeling, a space, a visual ambition, or a question about what kind of stone can carry the right atmosphere.

We welcome early-stage inquiries as much as developed project requests, provided the conversation is thoughtful and intentional.

Material Availability

For current Red Onyx discussions, slab and block questions, and project-fit conversations.

Private Viewing

For clients who wish to initiate a direct and selective material review.

Sourcing Requests

For projects requiring a particular tone, atmosphere, or rare stone beyond current signature availability.

Design Dialogue

For architects and designers seeking guidance on how material may best live within a space.
