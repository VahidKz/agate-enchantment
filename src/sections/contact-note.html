How We Prefer to Work

We value clarity, discretion, and project context from the beginning.

Agate Stone is not structured around anonymous requests or generic stock communication. We prefer a direct and thoughtful exchange that begins with the nature of the project and the role the material is expected to play.

This lets us respond with greater precision, whether the inquiry concerns Ember Vein, a private sourcing request, project-specific selection, or a conversation around form, finish, scale, and presence.
