How We Prefer to Work

We value clarity, discretion, and project context from the beginning.

Agate Enchantment is not structured around anonymous requests or generic stock communication. We prefer a more direct and thoughtful exchange: one that begins with the nature of the project, the desired atmosphere, and the role the material is expected to play.

This allows us to respond with greater precision — whether the inquiry concerns Red Onyx, a private sourcing request, a project-specific selection, or a conversation around form, finish, scale, and visual presence.

Private Inquiry

Tell us about the project, not only the product.

The more context you share, the more refined our response can be. Material dialogue begins with use, scale, tone, and intent.

What You Can Contact Us For

Not every inquiry begins with a finished specification.

Some conversations begin with a material. Others begin with a feeling, a space, a visual ambition, or a question about what kind of stone can carry the right atmosphere.

We welcome early-stage inquiries as much as more developed project requests — provided the conversation is thoughtful and intentional.
